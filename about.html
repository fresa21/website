About us

At Furlong Riding School you can enjoy the countryside South of Manchester. Here you can learn to ride a horse with qualified instructors who know what they are doing. There are riding courses for adults and for the children there is a Pony Club. You can have classes in general horse riding, jumping and dressage. There is even a gallop track for those who love living life in the fast lane.

Where to find us

Opening Hours

Tuesday - Friday 0900 - 2000

Saturday - Sunday 1000 - 1400

Mondays there are no classes as the horses need to rest.

Prices

Adults: £295 per quarter

Children: £220 per quarter

First trial class: £30

Should you not be able to attend class on a given day please let us know 24 hours in advance and we will arrange another time.

Equipment

Essential

A hard riding hat which meets current safety regulations

Jodpur boots or full length riding boots with a small heel. Trainers are not permitted.

Useful

Crop or dressage stick. Please note that the dressage stick is only for dressage classes
